Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez: Candidate for New York's 14th Congressional District

By Mizuki Uchiyama | October 6, 2024

Introduction

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez, also known as AOC, is the Democratic incumbent running for re-election in New York's 14th Congressional District. A member of the progressive wing of the party, she advocates for policies like the Green New Deal and Medicare for All. AOC will appear on the Democratic Party ticket in the November 2024 ballot.

Background

Born and raised in the Bronx, Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez was first elected to Congress in 2018 after a historic primary win against a long-time incumbent. She is a former educator and activist, advocating for working-class Americans. Read more

Climate Policies

AOC is a co-author of the Green New Deal, a sweeping policy proposal aiming to address climate change by transitioning the U.S. to 100% renewable energy and creating millions of jobs in clean energy industries. Learn more

Reproductive Health Policies

She is a staunch advocate for reproductive rights, supporting federal protections for abortion access and funding for Planned Parenthood. Ocasio-Cortez has called for overturning restrictive state laws that limit abortion services. Learn more

Immigrant Policies

As a representative of a diverse district, AOC supports a path to citizenship for undocumented immigrants and the abolishment of Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE). She pushes for comprehensive immigration reform that emphasizes human rights. Read more
